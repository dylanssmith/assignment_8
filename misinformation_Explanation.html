Misinformation

This Dark Pattern Monster combines two dark patterns, Confirshaming and Misdirection (as seen on https://darkpatterns.org/)

Confirmshaming is the act of guilting the user into opting into something. The option to decline is worded in such a way as to shame the user into compliance.

Misdirection is when the design purposefully focuses your attention on one thing in order to distract you attention from another.
